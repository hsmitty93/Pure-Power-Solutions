Joining Forces

While working together since the late 1990's our clients continued to ask for more energy efficient means of increasing their productivity and saving money. With our background in batteries, chargers and warehouse design we realized the need to utilize technology and energy utility rebates for many of our clients needs. We focused on industrial battery chargers, lighting, and air stratification fans and created Pure Power Solutions. We follow a process of collecting data from the clients existing operation with energy & verification tools; comparing that data with newer technology options and showing them a return on investment timeline by upgrading their operation. We believe educating the customer with data from inside their own walls using their own utility bills is more effective than selling them on the promises of marketing literature and the ROI's of ideal benchmarks derived from manufacturer claims.

Taking Action

Conserving energy and saving money in your warehouse by measuring your energy use with our proprietary Powermeter we can measure kWh use on your existing industrial battery chargers and upgrade you to new money saving ACT High Frequency chargers.


Find out how you can increase your profit margin by utilizing State and Federal energy incentives and rebates to allow you a return on investment as fast as 18 months to update your lights, upgrade your industrial forklift battery chargers, and install overhead HVLS fans and air stratification systems to reduce your heating and cooling costs.
